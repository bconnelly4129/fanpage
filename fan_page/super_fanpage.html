The DO'S of Disney

  • DO-Stay at a Disney resort
    • This will provied you with a full experince and can help you get little discounts in the park
    • Sometimes the resort can get you extra park hours!
    • The resorts also have fun activitiys for all ages!
  • DO-When staying at a Disney resort buy the disney fountain sovunier cups
    • They are around $20-$30 but you will get thursty so being able to fill up that cup anytime for no additonal charge is worth it
  • DO- Wear a watch that counts your steps
    • Some days you may be walking for 10 hours and that is usually around 20,000 steps.
    • It is intresting to see how mnay steps you complete in a day or even your whole trip.
  • DO- Arrive early in the parks.
    • The lines and security checks are super long so the earlier you get the smaller the line and earlier you can get into the park
    • Someimtes the park will open the gate early and if you are throguh security you can get a head start getting in line for the first ride
  • DO- Have the my Disney experaince app.
    • This app shows you all the wait times on the rides and if a ride is closed
    • It also has maps of the park and what resturants and restrooms are near.

The DON'TS of Disney

  • DON'T- forget your sneakers or comfortbale walking shoes
    • One time I forgot to bring sneakers and I had to walk around the park all day in crocs

    • These are not comfortable shoes to walk in!!

  • DON'T- Make a dining reservation in Epcot
    • The best thing to do food wise in Epcot is to eat in diffretn countires and get snacks.
    • There are many good meals, snacks, and desserts in every country in Epcot. You dont want to miss out!
  • DON'T-forget to bring water bottles with you in the park.
    • The water in the park is really expensive so if you have a water bottle with you, it could save you a lot of money.
